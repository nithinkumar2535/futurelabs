How our test process works!

Step 1

Vaccinated Phlebotomists

Only vaccinated phlebotomists are assigned orders to ensure safety.

Step 2

Maintains Safety Precautions

Phlebotomists wear masks, face shields, gloves, and sanitize before each visit.

Step 3

Sample Collection

Samples are collected using syringes and securely stored in barcoded vials.

Step 4

Sample Storage

Vials are kept in cooling containers to maintain the required temperature.

Step 5

High-Tech Facility

Samples are processed in 100% automated machines for precision and efficiency.

Step 6

Accurate Digital Reports

Clinically validated reports are generated and delivered digitally.
